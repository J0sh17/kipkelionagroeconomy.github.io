1.COFFEE GROWING HISTORY

The history of coffee in kenya is a complicated one and reflective of the country broader history as a British colony and the struggle for independence.All the coffee had to be centrally processed and marketed.The country best coffee was exported and only the poor quality coffee was sold locally.

Government restriction on the growing and selling of coffee were in place untill less than 15 years ago.
