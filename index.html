KIPKELION AGROECONOMY

Kipkelion sub-county in Kericho county is one of the rich places in agriculture and economic activities. It has a favorable climate and fertile soil that support crop growing and keeping dairy breed cows.

TYPES OF AGROECONOMIC ACTIVITIES

Kipkelion subcounty can be said to be one of the best places and favorable to practice agricultural activities. It has a favorable climate. The temperature in this area ranges between 23 to 28 degrees Celsius, and the rainfall is between 1500mm to 2500mm. This climate is favorable to grow different crops.

These agricultural activities include:

  • Coffee growing
  • Tea growing
  • Maize growing
  • Beans growing
  • Dairy keeping
  • Tomato tree growing
